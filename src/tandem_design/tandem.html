    Welcome to Tandem CA2 Looking after the planet.

    This is an overview of the tasks to be performed. Click to skip it and start the activity. You can refer back to this page later by clicking on the “Info” button in the top right-hand corner of the main page.

To advance in the different parts of the exercise one of you must make a request through the buttons of each task which must be confirmed by your partner.The message line at the bottom of your screen will indicate the activity status and progress.

Spot the difference

Both participants see the same image. In this image there are a series of numbered objects. Participants will take turns in describing these objects without naming them so that their task partners can guess which object it is.

Ranking task

Participants see the same image but with a number of differences. Describe your picture to your task partner and identify the differences. Every time you identify a difference both participants should click on the confirm difference button under the image.

Spot the difference

You and your task partner can see the same picture but with 3 differences. Describe the images to each other and identify the 3 hidden elements in one of the pictures. Once you have identified a missing element in the other participant's image click on it, and if you are right, this element will become visible to him/her.
